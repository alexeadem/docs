Effective Date: Thu Jul 24 12:58:40 AM PDT 2025

## Overview

This Acceptable Use Policy (“AUP”) defines the permitted and prohibited uses of the QBO platform. All users must comply with this policy when accessing or using QBO.

## Permitted Use

QBO may be used to run lawful workloads on authorized infrastructure in accordance with applicable agreements and system configurations.

## Prohibited Activities

Users may not use QBO to:

- Engage in illegal activity or violate any applicable laws or regulations
- Attempt unauthorized access to systems, networks, or data
- Interfere with or disrupt infrastructure, services, or other users
- Deploy malware, botnets, or malicious workloads
- Abuse, bypass, or attempt to circumvent platform controls or security mechanisms
- Use the platform in violation of export control or sanctions regulations
- Infrastructure and Resource Use

Users must operate within the defined hardware configuration and system limits associated with their deployment. Attempts to exceed, manipulate, or misuse allocated infrastructure are not permitted.

## Compliance

Users are responsible for ensuring that their use of QBO complies with all applicable legal, regulatory, and organizational requirements, including export controls and data handling policies.

## Enforcement

QBO reserves the right to investigate violations of this policy and to suspend or terminate access to the platform at its sole discretion.

## Updates

QBO may update this Acceptable Use Policy from time to time. Continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
